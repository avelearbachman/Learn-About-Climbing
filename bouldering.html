Boulder Climbing

Grading System

This type of climbing uses the V-Scale which which is named after a famous climber named John Vermin Sherman. The bouldering problems start at "V.0" and go to "V.17".
Bouldering is done with no ropes. The walls can range from ten to fifteen feet tall and once you fall you land on the mats. The mats are big enough to catch you but it's important to learn how to fall correctly.
