Speed Climbing

Grading System

This type of climbing doesn't have a grading system
This type of climbing is very different from the rest because it measures speed not how far you get on the route. This uses the same route for all of USA Climbing and is the same route that Olympians compete on. The fastest time for men is 4.90 seconds and the fastest for women's is 6.84 seconds. The wall is fifteen meters or in feet almost fifty.
